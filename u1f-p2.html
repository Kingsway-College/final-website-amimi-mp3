AUTOMATION


Arknights: Endfield's Factory system automates resource production, allowing players to set up production lines that generate materials over time. This system streamlines gameplay by providing a steady supply of resources with minimal effort, letting players focus on other aspects of the game.

I plan to focus on the Factory system in Arknights: Endfield to maximize material production. By optimizing production lines, I'll ensure a steady supply of resources to help with upgrades and crafting.
