My Plan for Arknights: Endfield



Arknights: Endfield is an upcoming open-world action-adventure game set in the Arknights universe. Players explore the planet Talos-II, managing resources and battling environmental threats caused by the Erosion phenomenon. It blends strategic combat with base-building elements. The game is currently in its first open beta phase.


In the coming months, I plan to dedicate time to exploring the world of Arknights: Endfield, focusing on farming resources and building up characters. I'll dive into the game's mechanics, strategically developing my base and enhancing my team to progress through its challenges.
